Understanding Staking & Borrowing

How Staking Works

  • Choose how much silver to stake
  • Your silver is securely vaulted
  • Unstake anytime after repaying any loans

How Borrowing Works

  • Stake your silver as collateral
  • Borrow up to 50% of staked value in USDST
  • Keep earning rewards while borrowed
  • Repay loan partially or fully anytime

Important Note

Your staked silver remains locked until any borrowed amount is repaid. You can repay your loan partially or fully at any time. Once repaid, you can unstake your silver immediately.
