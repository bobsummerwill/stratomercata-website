Important Note

USDST is the exclusive currency for loans and repayments in STRATO Mercata's lending system. When you borrow against staked assets, you receive USDST, and when repaying loans, you must use USDST. This creates a stable, predictable lending environment where loan amounts and repayments maintain consistent value.

USDST Lending Currency

USDST is STRATO Mercata's lending currency, used exclusively for borrowing against staked assets and loan repayments. Each USDST maintains a stable 1:1 peg to the US dollar.

Example: When you stake $1,000 worth of gold, you can borrow up to 500 USDST
